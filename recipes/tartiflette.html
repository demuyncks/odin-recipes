Tartiflette

Description

La fameuse tartiflette savoyarde is a typical and famous potato-based dish. We retrieve in various forms and tastes. Our is a delicious traditional-style tartiflette.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 1 reblochon cheese
  • 1 onion
  • white wine
  • 200 g bacon lardons
  • butter
  • 200 g cream

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes in salted water
  2. Pre-warm the oven to 200 degrees
  3. Cut the onion and cook it with lardons
  4. Add the wine and let it reduce
  5. Mix potatoes with onions, lardons and cream in a dish
  6. Place reblochon slices on top and bake for 25 minutes
