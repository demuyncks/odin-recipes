Lasagna

Description

La fabulosa lasagna italiana is a typical and famous wide pasta-based dish. We retrieve in various forms and tastes. Our is a delicious vegetarian-style lasagna.

Ingredients

  • lasagna pastas
  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 onion
  • pesto
  • 1 peperroni
  • maizena
  • 200 g cream

Steps

  1. Put pastas into water
  2. Pre-warm the oven to 200 degrees
  3. Cut the vegetables and bake it
  4. Mix the cream and 100g of maizena
  5. Put one layer of veggies, then one of pastas, then veggies, and so on...
  6. Put in the oven during 25 minutes
